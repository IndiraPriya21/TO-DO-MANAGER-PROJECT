Welcome to the To-Do Manager Manual!

This manual provides a comprehensive guide to using your To-Do Manager application. Organize your tasks, set deadlines, and manage your productivity with ease.

Key Features:

  • Task Creation: Easily add new tasks with descriptions, priorities (Top, Medium, Low), and deadlines.
  • Deadline Management: Keep track of upcoming deadlines with a dedicated notification system that alerts you to tasks due within 2 days.
  • Priority Filtering: Filter your tasks by priority to focus on what's most important.
  • Task Status: Mark tasks as done or pending to keep your list organized and track your progress.
  • User Profile Management: Update your personal information directly within the application.
  • Intuitive Interface: A clean and user-friendly interface designed for optimal productivity.

How to Use:

  • Adding a Task: Click the "Add New Task" button, fill in the details in the side panel, and click "Add Task".
  • Editing a Task: Click the "Edit" button next to any task, modify the details in the side panel, and click "Update Task".
  • Deleting a Task: Click the "Delete" button next to any task and confirm the deletion.
  • Filtering Tasks: Use the "Priority" and "Pending" buttons to filter your task list. Click "Show All" to reset filters.
  • Notifications: The bell icon will show a badge with the number of upcoming deadlines. Click it to view the full list in a pop-up panel.
  • Managing Profile: Click on your profile icon to open your profile details. Use "Edit" to modify, "Save" to confirm, or "Cancel" to revert changes.

We hope this To-Do Manager helps you stay organized and achieve your goals!
